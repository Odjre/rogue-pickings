Menu

Please keep in mind that our offerings change daily depending on which ingredients are available. Some of this week's menu items are:

Flaming Hummus & Falafel Salad

Mixture of fresh greens, cherry tomatoes, olive, marinated onion, falafel, pita points, and our top secret Flaming Hummus.

Sizzling Bean Burrito

12 inch flour tortilla (tortilla flavors may vary), black beans, rice, pico de gallo, Mexican cheese, lettuce.

Green Gloves Tamales

Tamales with roasted poblano peppers and spicy pepper jack cheese, served with a side of green chile.

Camote Chimichurri

Chipotle roasted sweet potatoes, black beans, avocado, sweet corn, and chimichurri.

Jacked Up BBQ Sandwich

Smoked jackfruit, jicama kale slaw, and chili-lime mayo, sandwiched between two large plantain "buns".

Dengu

Green lentils cooked in a blend of seasonings, curry, and coconut milk, served with rice and cabbage.
